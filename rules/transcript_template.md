### Glossary
- **Bounded Function:** $f:[a,b] \to \mathbb{R}$ is bounded if $\exists m,M \in \mathbb{R}$ such that $m \leq f(x) \leq M$ for all $x \in [a,b]$.
- **Supremum (sup):** Least upper bound of a set.
- **Infimum (inf):** Greatest lower bound of a set.
