# U01L01 - Meaning of Research (Notes)

## Key Ideas
- Research is a systematic and scientific investigation.
- It seeks new knowledge, not just information.
- Moves from known to unknown.

## Definitions
- Information: existing facts.
- Knowledge: insight obtained through research.
- Hypothesis: testable proposition.

## Characteristics of Good Research
- Systematic
- Logical
- Empirical
- Replicable
- Critical

## Research Process
1. Define problem  
2. Review literature  
3. Form hypothesis  
4. Choose method  
5. Collect data  
6. Analyze  
7. Conclude  

## Exam Tip
Research method = tools and techniques.  
Methodology = overall logic behind the research design.
