## Core Ideas
- Use common refinement of partitions to combine bounds for multiple integrators.
- Upper and lower sums behave linearly under addition of integrators.
- Positivity of \(f\) preserves positivity of lower sums, hence positivity of the integral.
- If \(f_2 - f_1 \ge 0\), apply non-negativity to obtain order relations.
