# U02L03 - Additivity and Linearity of the Integral (Notes)

## Key Points
- Additivity of integrator.
- Linearity of Riemann–Stieltjes integral.
- Scalar multiplication of integrator.

## Definitions and Notation
- f ∈ R_{alpha1} and f ∈ R_{alpha2}.
- alpha = alpha1 + alpha2.
- Δ alpha_i = alpha(x_i) - alpha(x_{i-1}).

## Core Results
- f ∈ R_{alpha1+alpha2}.
- ∫ f d(alpha1+alpha2) = ∫ f d alpha1 + ∫ f d alpha2.
- f ∈ R_{c alpha} and ∫ f d(c alpha) = c ∫ f d alpha.

## Important Derivations
- Upper and lower sums split into sums over alpha1 and alpha2.
- Each difference < ε/2 ensures combined < ε.

## Relationships
- Integrator additivity induces integral linearity.

## Summary
Lecture proves additivity and linearity properties for Riemann–Stieltjes integrals.
