📄 Math Symbols Cheat Sheet (Condensed)

A quick one-page reference for the most essential mathematical symbols.
All math uses Obsidian-friendly LaTeX delimiters (......... inline, ......... block).

Logic & Quantifiers​

  • ∀\forall∀ — for all. Example: ∀x∈R,x2≥0\forall x \in \mathbb{R}, x^2 \ge 0∀x∈R,x2≥0.
  • ∃\exists∃ — there exists. Example: ∃x:x2=4\exists x: x^2=4∃x:x2=4.
  • ∃!\exists!∃! — unique existence. Example: ∃!x:x+1=0\exists! x: x+1=0∃!x:x+1=0.
  • ⇒\Rightarrow⇒ — implies. P⇒QP \Rightarrow QP⇒Q.
  • ⇔\Leftrightarrow⇔ — iff (if and only if).

Sets​

  • x∈Ax \in Ax∈A — x in A.
  • x∉Ax \notin Ax∈/A — x not in A.
  • A⊆BA \subseteq BA⊆B — subset.
  • A∪BA \cup BA∪B — union.
  • A∩BA \cap BA∩B — intersection.
  • ∅\varnothing∅ — empty set.
  • ∣A∣|A|∣A∣ — size of A.

Relations & Order​

  • === equal, ≠\neq= not equal.
  • <<<, ≤\leq≤, >>>, ≥\geq≥ — inequalities.
  • a∣ba \mid ba∣b — a divides b.
  • a∤ba \nmid ba∤b — a does not divide b.
  • ≡\equiv≡ — congruent (mod).

Functions​

  • f:A→Bf:A\to Bf:A→B — f maps A to B.
  • f∘gf \circ gf∘g — composition.
  • f−1f^{-1}f−1 — inverse.
  • ker⁡f\ker fkerf — kernel.
  • im⁡f\operatorname{im} fimf — image.

Arithmetic & Combinatorics​

  • n!n!n! factorial.
  • (nk)\binom{n}{k}(kn​) — n choose k.
  • ∑\sum∑ — sum.
  • ∏\prod∏ — product.
  • ±\pm± plus/minus.

Algebra & Linear Algebra​

  • ATA^TAT transpose.
  • det⁡A\det AdetA determinant.
  • tr⁡A\operatorname{tr} AtrA trace.
  • ⟨x,y⟩\langle x,y \rangle⟨x,y⟩ inner product.
  • ∥x∥\|x\|∥x∥ norm.

Calculus & Analysis​

  • lim⁡f(x)\lim f(x)limf(x) — limit.
  • f′(x)f'(x)f′(x), ddxf\frac{d}{dx}fdxd​f — derivative.
  • ∂∂x\frac{\partial}{\partial x}∂x∂​ — partial derivative.
  • ∫f(x)dx\int f(x)dx∫f(x)dx — integral.
  • sup⁡,inf⁡\sup, \infsup,inf — sup/inf.
  • ⌊x⌋\lfloor x \rfloor⌊x⌋, ⌈x⌉\lceil x \rceil⌈x⌉ — floor/ceiling.

Topology & Metric Spaces​

  • A‾\overline{A}A closure.
  • int⁡(A)\operatorname{int}(A)int(A) interior.
  • ∂A\partial A∂A boundary.
  • d(x,y)d(x,y)d(x,y) distance.
  • B(x,r)B(x,r)B(x,r) open ball.

Probability & Measure​

  • P(A)\mathbb{P}(A)P(A) probability.
  • E[X]\mathbb{E}[X]E[X] expectation.
  • Var⁡(X)\operatorname{Var}(X)Var(X) variance.
  • LpL^pLp space.
  • f=gf=gf=g a.e. — equal almost everywhere.

Complex Analysis​

  • iii imaginary unit.
  • ℜz\Re zℜz, ℑz\Im zℑz — real/imag part.
  • ∣z∣|z|∣z∣ modulus.
  • arg⁡z\arg zargz argument.
  • Res(f,z0)\mathrm{Res}(f,z_0)Res(f,z0​) residue.

Differential Operators​

  • ∇f\nabla f∇f gradient.
  • ∇⋅F\nabla \cdot F∇⋅F divergence.
  • ∇×F\nabla \times F∇×F curl.
  • Δf\Delta fΔf Laplacian.

Asymptotics​

  • O(g(n))O(g(n))O(g(n)) big-O.
  • o(g(n))o(g(n))o(g(n)) little-o.
  • f∼gf \sim gf∼g asymptotic equivalence.
  • Θ,Ω\Theta, \OmegaΘ,Ω — growth notations.

Proof​

  • □\square□ — QED (end of proof).
  • ∴\therefore∴ — therefore.

End of Cheat Sheet
